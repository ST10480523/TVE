About The.Ville.Era

The.VILLE.ERA are a 3 piece South Africa Hip Hop band from Johannesburg, South Africa. The band formed in 2016 from a collection of Just Chilled NIGGUHS who met at Johannesburg and who all just couldn't get enough Kendrick, Drake, Travis Scott. Their unique brand of entertainment fuses Battle Raps and Killer Punches with WILD and CRAZY that will make you want to REFLECT. create music that moves people and represents our journey.
